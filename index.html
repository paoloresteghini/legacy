Our Values

Guiding every
step we take.

Our mission is to make nutrition, fitness, and healthy living accessible, intentional, and sustainable.

These values guide the way we coach, support, and empower our clients to pursue lifelong health.

We believe in caring for our bodies with intention and gratitude — recognizing health as something to be cultivated, not taken for granted.

We lead with guidance, not dependency. Our role is to educate and coach — helping you build the skills and confidence to make informed choices long term.

Progress is built on consistency and consistency builds legacy. We walk alongside you, providing honest feedback, encouragement, and accountability to help you stay committed.

We focus on long-term transformation rooted in behavioral change — not shortcuts, quick fixes, or unsustainable extremes.

Meet the Team

We are faith driven, family centered, and dedicated to helping others.

AK

Dr. Ali Kassamali

Head Nutritionist

Ali is a double board-certified physician with a background in Economics and Psychology from Emory University, post-baccalaureate studies at NYU, and medical training at the University of Miami Miller School of Medicine.

After completing his residency in Anesthesiology and fellowship in Interventional Pain Management, he spent ten years treating patients with chronic pain and spine conditions. During those years, Ali saw just how many health issues could have been prevented with better nutrition, movement, and lifestyle habits.

Today, Ali blends his medical expertise, experience as a certified trainer, and a deep understanding of physiology to design personalized fitness and nutrition programs for people of all ages and backgrounds—from athletes and busy parents to seniors working to improve their labs and quality of life.

His goal is simple: empower clients to feel more confident, prevent chronic illness, and live healthier lives.

LK

Lauren Kassamali

Certified Trainer & Operations

Lauren is a certified trainer with a lifelong love of fitness and a heart for helping people feel healthier and more confident. She earned her BSBA from the University of Florida in marketing, communications, and entrepreneurship.

Her athletic background began at age three in a mom-and-tot gymnastics class and grew into a decade of competitive gymnastics, followed by cheerleading, diving, and eventually cheering at the University of Florida. As an adult, she found a new passion in CrossFit and the community it brought.

Her health journey deepened at age 25, when a breast cancer diagnosis reshaped her view of the role nutrition plays in overall wellness.

Today, Lauren channels her passion for health into the heart of Legacy Training's operations, client experience, and community building.
